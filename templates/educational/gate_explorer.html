Key Concept: Quantum State Evolution

Each quantum gate performs a unitary transformation on a quantum state. By exploring these transformations, you can understand how quantum algorithms manipulate information at the fundamental level.

Common Quantum States:
  • |0> - Qubit in ground state
  • |1> - Qubit in excited state
  • |+> = (|0> + |1>)/sqrt(2) - Superposition state
  • |-> = (|0> - |1>)/sqrt(2) - Negative superposition
  • |i+>, |i-> - Complex superposition states
Gate Categories:
  • Pauli Gates: X, Y, Z (bit flip, phase flip)
  • Hadamard: Creates superposition from basis states
  • Phase Gates: S, T (add phase without changing probabilities)
  • Rotation Gates: Rx, Ry, Rz (continuous rotations)
